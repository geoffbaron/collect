Privacy Policy

Collect iOS App  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Collect ("we", "the app") is a room asset cataloging app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what we share.

Camera & Microphone

Collect requests access to your device's camera and microphone solely to record room videos for asset analysis. Video is processed on-device (frame extraction) and then sent to the Google Gemini API for analysis. No video is stored on our servers. Video files are deleted from your device immediately after analysis is complete.

Data Sent to Third Parties

Extracted video frames (still images) are sent to the Google Gemini API for analysis. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No other data is sent to third parties.

Data Stored on Your Device

All property, room, and asset data you create is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to this data. It is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

API Key

Your Gemini API key is stored locally in your device's secure UserDefaults storage. It is never transmitted to or stored by us.

Account Information

If you create an account, your email address and display name are stored locally on your device only. We do not operate servers that store account information.

Analytics & Tracking

Collect does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

Collect is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
